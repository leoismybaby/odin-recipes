Tonkatsu

Juicy delicious tonkatsu.

Tonkatsu is a delicious Japanese dish consisting of breaded and fried pork cutlets. Boneless pork loins or pork chops are dredged in flour and egg, then coated in Japanese bread crumbs called 'panko'. It's typically served with tonkatsu sauce, thinly shredded cabbage, and steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Pork loins
  • Eggs
  • All purpose flour
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  • Place all purpose flour and panko crumbs in their own bowls. Crack and beat egg in another bowl.
  • Season both sides of pork loin with salt and pepper.
  • Coat pork loin with flour. Make sure to pack it in there. Dip in egg wash, then coat with panko bread crumbs.
  • Repeat with remaining pork loins.
  • Deep fry pork loins until golden and crispy. Cut into pieces and enjoy!
