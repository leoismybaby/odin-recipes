Miso Salmon

A photo of delicious miso salmon.

Salmon fillets marinated in a mixture of miso, sake, and soy sauce, then cooked under the broiler.

This miso salmon is fresh fish fillets soaked in a flavorful marinade, then broiled to perfection and topped with sesame seeds. A unique and delicious main course option that takes just minutes to put together!

I'm looking for fresh ways to serve up salmon, I turn to family favorite salmon recipes such as almond crusted salmon, honey glazed salmon, broiled salmon and this flavorful miso salmon. Serve your salmon with a side of sushi rice and watch the rave reviews come in!

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp Miso paste
  • 1 tbsp Sake
  • 1 tbsp Mirin
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
Steps
  1. To make marinade, combine miso paste, sake, mirin, and soy sauce in a bowl or container.
  2. Place salmon fillets face down into marinade. Spoon onto the skin.
  3. After marinading for 3 hours, place in oven or air fryer until meat is no longer pink inside.
